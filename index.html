How Many People Are Pirating Music?

Downloading and torrenting music illegally has been common since the dawn of the modern internet, but recently ripping copyrighted music from a streaming source has become increasingly popular. IFPI data shows stream ripping has actually overtaken illegal downloads as of 2016.

Type % of Web Users
Any 35%
Stream Ripping 30%
Downloading 19%

Effects of Piracy

Piracy takes away monetary incentive to create music, as piracy allows users to bypass all costs and purchases meaning no money is made that can be paid to the creator.
